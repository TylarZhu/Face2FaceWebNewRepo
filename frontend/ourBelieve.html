Our Beliefs

What we believe about God, Jesus, the Bible, the Gospel, and the Church.

GOD

The Triune God of Grace

We believe in one eternal God who is the Creator of all things. He exists in three Persons: God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit. He is totally loving and completely holy.

JESUS

The Centrality of Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ is at the center of everything. We believe that Jesus, who is fully God and fully human, is the only one who can reconcile us to God. He lived a sinless and exemplary life, died on the cross in our place, and rose again to prove His victory and empower us for life. We believe that Jesus Christ is coming back again as He promised.

BIBLE

The Revelation and Authority of Holy Scripture

The Bible is Godâ€™s Spirit-inspired word to us and is authoritative over every part of our lives. It is one unified story that points to Jesus. It reveals who God is and who we are as it unfolds the good news about Jesus and his redemptive work.

GOSPEL

The Good News of Redemption Through Jesus

This is the message unfolded in the story of the Bible: that God is at work in redeeming our broken and sinful world through his son Jesus in stunningly beautiful and powerful ways, and we are invited to receive, announce, and participate in his redemptive work.

CHURCH

The Spirit-Empowered Community of Jesus

The Church is the community of Jesus' followers that is created, sustained, and empowered by the indwelling Holy Spirit of God. God gives the Church various gifts so that we can be faithfully engaged and fully devoted disciples of Jesus.
